Dork Diaries by Rachel Renée Russell

The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins is set in a dystopian future where the oppressive Capitol forces

each of the 12 districts to send two tributes to fight to the death in a televised event. The story follows

Katniss Everdeen from District 12, who volunteers to take her sister's place in the deadly competition.

Alongside fellow tribute Peeta Mellark, Katniss must survive and challenge the Capitol’s control.
