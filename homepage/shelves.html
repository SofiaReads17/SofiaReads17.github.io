Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins

The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins is set in a dystopian future where the oppressive Capitol forces

each of the 12 districts to send two tributes to fight to the death in a televised event. The story follows

Katniss Everdeen from District 12, who volunteers to take her sister's place in the deadly competition.

Alongside fellow tribute Peeta Mellark, Katniss must survive and challenge the Capitol’s control.

CHRISTIAN: No

AGE: 13+; Contains violence and sensitive topics

Guts by Raina Telgemeier

Guts is a graphic novel by Raina Telgemeier featuring a young girl named Raina as she navigates fear,

school, and growing up. She deals with stomach troubles that turn out to be connected to her emotions,

making everyday life a challenge. Raina’s honesty and humor make her story relatable, as she learns to

ace her fears and speak openly about mental health, all while trying to be a normal kid.

CHRISTIAN: No

AGE: 10+; Contains sensitive topics

Oliver Twist by Charles Dickens by Author

Oliver Twist is a novel by Charles Dickens featuring a poor, orphaned boy named Oliver. He endures a

harsh life in a workhouse, runs away to London, and gets entangled with a gang of thieves led by Fagin.

Oliver’s innocence and resilience make him the heart of the story, as he navigates a world of crime and

cruelty, yet still finds kindness and hope through the people who truly care for him.

CHRISTIAN: Yes, there are are many Christian themes and characters.

AGE: 14+; Contains violent or disturbing content and mature topics
